PERFORMANCES

NIGERIAN IDOLS SEASON 9

Performed as both an acoustic and electric guitarist on Nigerian Idols Season 9, one of Africa’s leading music talent shows. Collaborated with celebrity guest artists such as Simi and Chike, delivering captivating live performances that fused contemporary pop, soul, and African rhythms. Worked closely with contestants, music directors, and producers to craft expressive stage moments and elevate the emotional impact of each performance. Demonstrated musical versatility, refined tone control, and confident stage presence before a nationwide audience.

  • https://youtube.com/playlist?list=PLu_6cjjNhvfsSqqioqMorFcO5a76JQqNt&si=-RWEErhKZSurNAv-

JOEBOY LAVIDA SESSIONS

One of my standout collaborations was the Lavida Sessions, a globally streamed acoustic reimagination of Joeboy’s catalog, hosted on his official YouTube channel.I composed and performed original African guitar lines that blended highlife, palmwine textures, and Afrobeat rhythms into Joeboy’s soulful pop vocals — giving each track a new sonic life. These guitar arrangements were purpose-built for live performance and became central to the mood and tone of the session. The session remains globally accessible and reflects my ability to contribute to international-quality productions.

  • https://youtu.be/9yeaAC0_zxY?si=iwPPq-QLtyJRNe2_-

ZAM - UCHENNA OKEREKE

Collaborated as the principal guitarist for Nigerian gospel praise artist Uchenna Okereke, contributing to multiple live-recorded projects such as “Nigeria Dialect Praise”, “African Worship Medley”,“Africa Praise Medley” and "Zam". Delivered expressive African-style guitar work characterized by intricate rhythmic phrasing and melodic motifs, performed live without overdubs. Played a key role in shaping the artist’s sound during national tours and major concerts across Nigeria.

  • https://youtube.com/playlist?list=PLu_6cjjNhvfsSqqioqMorFcO5a76JQqNt&si=-RWEErhKZSurNAv-

SEE AS YOU DEY DO ME SO - OLAITAN DADA

I collaborated with acclaimed music producer and artist Olaitan Dada, contributing both in studio and on stage. I recorded and tracked all guitar parts for his single “See As You Dey Do Me,” delivering expressive guitar arrangements that enhanced the song’s acoustic and rhythmic foundation. My contribution supported the song’s production quality and musical depth, working under professional studio conditions to meet high creative standards.

  • https://youtu.be/uTqiZyrgx7U?si=70jCW1lDEMGBnBUR

YOUR LOVE - DIFFERENT VOICES WORSHIP

I am the lead guitarist for Different Voices Worship Collective, a contemporary gospel group dedicated to producing spirit-led, original worship music. I recorded and arranged all live guitar parts for the collective’s debut project, infusing each song with dynamic phrasing, tone layering, and rhythmic depth. My guitar work was integral to shaping the overall musical identity of the project.Notably, I co-produced the track “Your Love,” a worship ballad centered on the theme of divine love. I composed the song’s signature guitar intro and contributed to both the sonic architecture and emotional progression of the piece.

  • https://youtube.com/playlist?list=PLu_6cjjNhvfsSqqioqMorFcO5a76JQqNt&si=-RWEErhKZSurNAv-
