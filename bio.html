Bio

Uchenna Ogbu, professionally known as “Uchenna”, is a Nigerian guitarist, record producer, and composer celebrated for his genre-blending style that fuses soul, contemporary jazz, blues, and afropop into emotionally rich soundscapes.

A seasoned session and live guitarist, Uchenna has performed and recorded with some of Nigeria’s most respected artists, including Brymo, Joeboy, Dremo, GUC, Olusayo Orodele, Naomi Mac, Olaitan Dada, Canvas N Clay, Tomi Thomas, and many others. He has also collaborated with several of Nigeria’s major record labels, lending his distinctive sound to studio sessions, live recordings, concerts, and televised performances most notably as the lead and acoustic guitarist on Nigerian Idol Season 9, where he showcased remarkable versatility across Afrobeats, jazz, dance, pop, gospel, and rock.

Uchenna’s artistry has graced major stages across Nigeria, touring with a wide range of artists and performing before thousands at landmark gospel and contemporary music events. His performances are defined by expressive phrasing, tonal depth, and an ability to connect deeply with audiences.

Beyond performance, Uchenna is a passionate music instructor, having trained both aspiring and established musicians including BBO, Grace Ichie and Rejoice Eweama through his personalized guitar mentorship program. His teaching focuses on musicality, technique, and creative expression, helping students discover their unique voice through the instrument.

As a producer and collaborator, Uchenna is known for crafting ambient textures, soulful tones, and immersive soundscapes using advanced multi-effects processors. His unique blend of African rhythmic sensibility and global musicality continues to position him as one of the most versatile and emotionally resonant guitarists in Nigeria’s contemporary music scene. With a growing discography, notable production credits, and a reputation for memorable live performances, Uchenna stands as part of the new wave of African musicians shaping the continent’s creative future on the global stage.

Born in Lagos, Nigeria, Uchenna discovered his passion for music during his undergraduate years at the University of Lagos. Outside of music, he enjoys food, travel, and maintaining an active lifestyle and looks forward to one day updating this bio to say he’s happily married to the love of his life and blessed with twins. .
