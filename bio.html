Bio

Uchenna Ogbu, professionally known as “Uchenna”, is a guitarist, producer, instructor and composer based in Lagos, Nigeria. He is celebrated for his genre-blending style that fuses soul, contemporary jazz, blues, and afropop into emotionally rich soundscapes.

A seasoned session and live guitarist, Uchenna has performed and recorded with some of Nigeria’s most respected artists, including Brymo, Joeboy, Chike, Dremo, GUC, Olusayo Orodele, Naomi Mac, Uchenna Okereke, Olaitan Dada, Canvas N Clay, Tomi Thomas, Pelumi Deborah, Amaeya, UAX, Wavy the creator and many others. He has also collaborated with several of Nigeria’s major record labels, lending his distinctive sound to studio sessions, live recordings, concerts, and televised performances most notably as the lead and acoustic guitarist on Nigerian Idol Season 9, where he showcased remarkable versatility across Afrobeats, jazz, dance, pop, gospel, and rock.

Uchenna’s artistry has graced major stages across Nigeria, touring with a wide range of artists and performing before thousands at landmark gospel and contemporary music events. His performances are defined by expressive phrasing, tonal depth, and an ability to connect deeply with audiences.

Uchenna also released his original single “Resolve,” showcasing his expressive guitar voice and musical depth. He is the creator of Uchenna’s Acoustic Diary (UAD), a creative series that highlights intimate guitar performances, musical storytelling, and his evolving artistic journey.

Beyond performance, Uchenna is a passionate music instructor, having trained both aspiring and established musicians including BBO, Grace Ichie and Rejoice Eweama through his personalized guitar mentorship program. His teaching focuses on musicality, technique, and creative expression, helping students discover their unique voice through the instrument.

As a producer and collaborator, Uchenna is known for crafting ambient textures, soulful tones, and immersive soundscapes, often shaped through advanced multi-effects processors. His work reflects a unique blend of African rhythmic sensibility and global musical influences. He co-produced Your Love (2025) by The Different Voices Worship Collective and You Know My Heart (2025) by Grace Ichie, and has also tracked guitars for numerous Nigerian artists across both the gospel and contemporary music scenes.

With a growing discography, notable production credits, and a reputation for memorable live performances, Uchenna continues to establish himself as one of the most versatile and emotionally resonant guitarists in Nigeria’s contemporary music landscape, part of a new wave of African musicians shaping the continent’s creative future on the global stage.

On a personal note, Uchenna was born in Lagos, Nigeria. He discovered his passion for music during his undergraduate years at the University of Lagos. Beyond music, he enjoys cooking and experimenting with new recipes, traveling, and maintaining an active lifestyle. He also looks forward to the day he can update this bio to say he is happily married to the love of his life and blessed with twins. .
