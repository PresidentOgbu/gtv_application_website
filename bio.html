Bio

Uchenna Ogbu, professionally known as Uchenna, is a Lagos-based guitarist, producer, composer, and instructor known for blending soul, contemporary jazz, blues, and Afropop into expressive, emotionally driven guitar performances.

A respected session and live guitarist, he has performed and recorded with leading Nigerian artists including Brymo, Joeboy, Chike, Dremo, GUC, Naomi Mac, Tomi Thomas, Wavy The Creator, and others. He also served as lead and acoustic guitarist on Nigerian Idol Season 9, demonstrating versatility across Afrobeats, jazz, gospel, pop, and rock.

Uchenna released his original single “Resolve” and created Uchenna’s Acoustic Diary (UAD), a series showcasing intimate guitar performances and musical storytelling. As a producer and collaborator, he has contributed to projects such as “Your Love” (2025) by The Different Voices Worship Collective and “You Know My Heart” (2025) by Grace Ichie, while continuing to record guitars for artists across Nigeria’s gospel and contemporary music scenes.

Through his performances, recordings, and mentorship of emerging musicians, Uchenna is building a reputation as one of the distinctive contemporary guitar voices emerging from Nigeria’s modern music landscape.
