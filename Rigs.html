Rigs

Every sound begins with the right tools. Over the years, I’ve developed a setup that allows me to move easily between ambient textures, soulful melodies, and rhythm-driven guitar work across gospel, contemporary, and alternative music. My approach to gear is simple: clarity, flexibility, and musical expression. Rather than relying on excessive equipment, I focus on tools that allow me to shape tone quickly in both live performance and studio recording environments.

Gear information

  • Processor: BOSS GT-100
  • Electric Guitar: Yamaha Pacifica , Squier Stratocaster by Fender

Every piece of gear in this setup serves one purpose to help translate ideas into sound. The focus is always on expression, musicality, and creating moments that resonate with the listener.

Uchenna is a guitarist, producer, composer, and instructor exploring ambient textures, expressive improvisation, and contemporary African guitar expression across multiple genres through performances, recordings, and collaborative projects.
